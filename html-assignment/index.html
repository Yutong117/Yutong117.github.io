About me

Hello, everyone. My name is Yutong Liu. I'm from China. I am a student in Algonquin College.I came to Ottawa at September 1st and this is my first year. I'm very excited to study IMD program. I look forward to not only improving my design ability through learning, but also constantly innovating in actual projects.
